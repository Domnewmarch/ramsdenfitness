1-1 Personal Training

This is our gold standard service. We offer face to face or online sessions. Our sessions are so much more than just seeing your PT every week. It means unparalleled support and motivation to achieve your health and fitness goals. From our detailed assessment we design a fully individualised programme based on your current fitness experience, strengths, weaknesses and your lifestyle. By working together face to face optimal results can be achieved quickly. Not only do we motivate and inspire you to work harder we can instantly adjust your programme or specific exercise technique to make it more effective. With our educational support programme you will gain the knowledge to build a solid foundation of exercise and nutrition. This will enable you to build a future healthier lifestyle. Weekly check in’s will ensure communication outside of your training sessions to ensure you are on track, staying accountable and to achieve your goals. At RF we are always here to guide and support you. We want your sessions to be enjoyable, rewarding and ultimately help you achieve your results to feel great! Group Training

At RF we offer both indoor and outdoor group training. Group training offers a different experience. Some people prefer to workout in a group environment where motivation from others can help. It also acts as a great add on to your other workouts to keep the variety and interest in your exercise sessions. The group training workouts are programmed weekly. Containing both a mixture of strength & endurance training alongside mobility & movement drills. I truly believe that everyone can benefit from these type of sessions. As well as the immense sessions & the amazing feeling you’ll feel afterwards, you also receive external nutritional support on top of that. If you would like to enquire about group training then press the “contact us” button.
